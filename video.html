RIDGE CLUB: Ridge (Refugees and Immigrants Devoted to Growth and Excellence) is a cultural club where you get to learn more about different cultures, languages, and food. In my experience Ridge club has been a good place for networking and it is also a great help for your personal growth beacuse you get a chance to think for others and help people in need. I believe Ridge is a great way to do something for the community espically the refugees or/and people with different background who are new to the country and speaks English as their second or third language.
